What I offer.

Music Production

With a strong understanding of brand and sonic aesthetic, I make sure I'm ‘in tune’ with your vision from the very beginning. My goal is to draw out the very best from artists and their songs and come out with the strongest sound possible.

Sound Design

I developed my foley and sound design skills working on a diverse range of projects. Ultimately, I understand that a great mix is a combination of technicality, creativity and presentation to create a truly impactful sound.

Audio Engineering

Mastering the latest DAW tools over almost a decade, I have been able to refine my skills recording, mixing and producing projects in Logic Pro, Pro Tools, Cubase and Ableton Live. I also has a wealth of experience as a field audio engineer for TV and audiovisual projects.

About

Hi there. I'm Hervé, a professional sound engineer and music producer. With experience spanning nearly a decade, I started my career composing music for promotional videos. I have been lucky enough to work with leading brands and organisations worldwide, including Maguen, Nike and Mango. More recently, I have been producing up-and-coming artists in one of the most vibrant music capitals in the world: London.

I feel I am a versatile producer, mainly working in urban styles such as hip hop, trap, r&b and pop, but that doesn't stop me from constantly approaching other genres with an experimental and forward-thinking attitude.

In my spare time, I have been working on a new project: DCHANGEE. Read more here.
